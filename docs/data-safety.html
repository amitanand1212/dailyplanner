Data Safety

Daily Planner · Last updated: June 22, 2026

Here's a clear summary of how Daily Planner handles your data. Your tasks stay on your device; the only data that leaves the app is what Google AdMob needs to show ads.

🔒 Your tasks stay on your device

The app has no server of its own. Your tasks, profile and settings are saved only on your device and are never sent to us.

📣 Ads use Google AdMob

To show ads, Google AdMob may collect your device's advertising ID and basic device info. This is the only data that leaves the app, and Google handles it.

🔔 Notifications stay on-device

Reminders are scheduled locally on your phone. They are not processed on any server.

🗑️ You control your data

Delete everything anytime with Reset Data in Profile, or by uninstalling the app.

This summary matches the official Data Safety details shown on our Google Play listing. Full details are in our Privacy Policy.
